Riot Invasion

Domain: 9-12
Team(s) Per School: 1
Participant(s) Per Team: 5+1(substitute)
Event Head: Rabnur Singh Jolly
Maximum Teams: 32

Guidelines:
All participants need to join the discord server for live-communication purposes.
Any form of cheating would lead to immediate disqualification of the team.
All players will be monitored from the server as well as individually spectated.
Players should have a strong and consistent internet connection. Excuses like lag will not be considered.
Since Riot Invasion is an online event, participants will use their own equipment. We will NOT be responsible for faulty software or hardware in any way.

Event Format:
The event will consist of 5v5 Valorant customs, same ruling as competitive. With exception of 1 technical timeout (5 minutes) and 1 tactical timeout (2 minutes) per team
All the matches will be knockouts.
The Finals will be Best Of 3.

About AzTech

AzTech was an initiative taken by 4 students. Formed in the summer of 2021, it is the Tech Community of K.R. Mangalam World School, Vikaspuri. The club which is a safe and knowledgable environment helps the enthusiasts explore their interests in Information Technology. The club provides enthusiasts with a wide variety of opportunities to showcase their talents in various technological fields.
